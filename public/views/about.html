About Me.

I am a software developer based in the Tempe, Arizona area.
I specialize in full stack development.

Ever since I was a small boy I always had a fascination with computers. My goal, as a child, was to try and click on everything I could and see what it would do. Eventually this drove my parents nuts and they started telling me to take breaks from using the computer and "Stop BREAKING it!"

Unforuntely for them, me being a small rambucious young boy, this only incouraged me to want to use it more. This was the beginning of my fascination with computers.

Growing up out in the "sticks" of Newman Lake, Washington (No.. this is not anywhere near Seattle!). Graduating college with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from Gonzaga University. And now moving to a large city where technology is booming. This has been my past but it has only paved a way for my ambitious spirit to want to continue to explore and learn new things.
